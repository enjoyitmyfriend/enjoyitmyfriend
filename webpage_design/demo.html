Superheroes Are Awesome!

Welcome to our webpage about superheroes and how amazing they are. We have included information about different superheroes as well as lots of other interesting stuff. We hope you enjoy our website a lot and that you see your favourite superhero on it.

If you don't see your favourite superhero you could contact us and tell us about them. We could then add them to our webpage so other people could find out about them too.

Superheroes have many different super powers. Most of them have the same aim though, to protect the public and to fight crime. Most superheroes will have a villain as their nemesis, someone they are always trying to save the world from.

The word superhero is a very old word and can be dated back to as early as 1917. Most of the superheroes we know today were created in comic books. Some of these date back to as early as the 1930s.
