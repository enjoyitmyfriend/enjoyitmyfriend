Linabell is a curious and inquisitive fox with a fluffy tail and an orchid adorning one of her pointed ears.
Equipped with her signature magnifying glass, she enjoys the thrill of solving problems and mysteries.
LinaBell also loves nature and spending time with Duffy and his friends in the forest.



Background Story:
One day, on an outing, Mickey and Duffy got separated following butterflies.
A fox passed by, "What's wrong?"
"I can't find my way back"
"Hmm. Sticky seed. Butterfly. Orchid. Got it. I can bring you back!"
"Wow, you're amazing!"
Excited to be reunited with Mickey, Duffy said, "She is really smart!", and gave her an orchid. "My name is LinaBell and I love solving mysteries."
