Plot

The AOTU competition is held every three years on the AOTU planet.

Here, the contestants can obtain the extraordinary ability given by the creators of AOTU - all kinds of powerful, strange and unique skills with the magic, or energy, called "Yuan Li" (元力, also META).

Through constant competition and fighting, the final winner will grand all his wishes by The Creator of AOTU.

Aotu World is a fast-paced mobile strategy RPG. Make a statement by styling your own trendy and colorful outfits!

Experience all new, fast-paced tactical battles.

Explore and unlock the ultimate secrets of Aotu World!

Pilot episode 0 part 1 and 2 are aired on November 2015, Season 1 is officially aired on 28th June 2016 on various platforms with a total of 32 episodes. Season 2 aired on 8th October 2017 at 10 in the morning with a total of 20 episodes.

Season 3 aired every Friday at 12 in the noon since 25th January 2019.
