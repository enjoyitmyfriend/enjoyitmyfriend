王者荣耀

王者荣耀 English name is HONOR OF KING.

王者荣耀Is a Multiplayer Online Battle Arena.

Many people are playing honor of king.(There's about 100million people playing this game).

The gameplay in Glory of the King is mainly based on competitive battles. Players can conduct PVP battles in 1VS1, 3VS3, 5VS5 and other ways.

They can also participate in the game's adventure mode and PVE mode.

After meeting the conditions, you can participate in the game. Qualifying matches, etc., are tower-pushing games.

"Honor of Kings" is a MOBA domestic mobile game developed and run by Tencent Games Tianmei Studio Group and operated on Android, IOS and NS platforms.

It was officially tested on Android and iOS platforms on November 26, 2015.

The names used in the early stage of the game are "Heroes of War" and "League of Kings".

"Arena Of Valor", the European and American version of "Honor of Kings", was released on the Nintendo Switch platform in 2018

The official server of the game (Android and IOS platforms) has released 109 characters, and new heroes will be launched regularly.

Hero positioning can be divided into mage, warrior, tank, assassin, shooter, and support.

Different heroes have different attributes and skills.

Each hero has multiple active attack skills and 1 passive skill.

In the matching mode, players can use weekly free heroes, experience card heroes and purchased heroes to participate in the battle.
