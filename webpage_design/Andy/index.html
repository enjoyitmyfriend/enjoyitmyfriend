Electronic Arts is an American multinational interactive entertainment software production and distribution company founded by Tripp Hawkins on May 28, 1982. There are branches or subsidiaries in China and other regions, and the total number of employees around the world exceeds 9,700. It is mainly engaged in the development, publishing and sales of computer games, console games, online games, and mobile games.
