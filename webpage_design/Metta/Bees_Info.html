Charity event information

We hold a monthly charity event at a local school. The event is a food market that allows local bee keepers to sell their honey and any homemade goodies such as cakes and sweet treats.

We would love to see you at our next event!

We also hold a yearly charity event that children can attend and play lots of games to learn about the importance of bees to our society.

We will post further information about this event at a later stage.
